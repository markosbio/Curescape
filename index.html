Our Prototypes

Sickle Cell Relief Syrup

Child-friendly, honey-flavoured supportive care syrup designed to promote overall wellbeing, organ health, and reduce the frequency of pain crises in individuals with sickle cell disease.

Cure Wound Gel

Plant-based topical gel formulated to support healing in diabetic and chronic wounds by maintaining a protective wound environment and promoting tissue repair.

Cure Wound Spray

Rapid-action wound spray providing antimicrobial protection, pain relief, and support for tissue recovery through a sterile, easy-to-apply format.
