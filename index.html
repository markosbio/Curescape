Our Prototypes

Sickle Cell Relief Syrup

A child-friendly, honey-flavoured supportive care syrup designed to assist pain management, organ health, and overall wellbeing for individuals living with sickle cell disease.

Cure Wound Gel

A plant-based topical gel formulated to support healing in diabetic and chronic wounds by maintaining a protective wound environment and promoting tissue repair.

Cure Wound Spray

A rapid-action wound spray providing antimicrobial protection, pain relief, and support for tissue recovery through a sterile, easy-to-apply format.
